Omelette

Description

Learn how to make the perfect, fluffy Omelette. Follow these simple tricks to avoid overcooking and ensure they are soft and tender. Once you learn how to make a basic Omelette, there are so many ways to customize it.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Beat eggs
  2. Heat skillet
  3. Pull cooked eggs
  4. Flip
  5. Fold
